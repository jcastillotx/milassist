## ‚úàÔ∏è Flight Search

### Using Google Flights Integration

The MilAssist platform integrates with Google Flights to provide comprehensive flight search capabilities.

#### Search Parameters

- **Origin**: Departure city or airport
- **Destination**: Arrival city or airport  
- **Dates**: Departure and return dates
- **Passengers**: Number of travelers
- **Class**: Economy, Business, or First class
- **Preferences**: Direct flights, specific airlines, etc.

#### Search Results

Each flight search returns:
- **Price**: Total cost including taxes and fees
- **Timing**: Departure and arrival times
- **Airline**: Operating carrier(s)
- **Stops**: Number and duration of layovers
- **Duration**: Total travel time
- **Booking Link**: Direct link to book the flight

### Advanced Search Options

#### Flexible Dates
- Search across a date range for best prices
- View price trends and recommendations
- Get notified of price drops

#### Multi-City Trips
- Plan complex itineraries
- Add multiple destinations
- Optimize routing and timing

#### Price Alerts
- Set up price monitoring
- Receive notifications for changes
- Track price history
